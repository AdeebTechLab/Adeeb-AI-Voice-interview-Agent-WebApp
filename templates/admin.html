One link for every applicant

Universal candidate link

Send the same secure link to every internee. A candidate enters their own full name, father / guardian name and CNIC. The system creates one record per unique CNIC.

The full CNIC and father / guardian name are visible only after HR signs in. They never appear in the shared candidate link. A candidate receives a resume code for an unfinished interview.

Meeting-agent behavior

Professional candidate flow

  1. The candidate enters their own name and gives recording consent.
  2. Adeeb asks questions in English and listens automatically.
  3. The candidate may answer in English, Urdu, Hindi, or mixed speech.
  4. The agent can repeat, answer approved FAQ questions, ask a relevant follow-up, or move forward when it hears “next question.”
  5. HR reviews the evidence-based English transcript and summary.
