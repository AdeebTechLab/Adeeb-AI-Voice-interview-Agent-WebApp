# Approved interview information

Use this file for verified details that the agent may share with a candidate. Adeeb searches this file together with the selected FAQ source. Keep facts current and brief.

## Adeeb AI Meeting Agent rules
- The agent hosts a structured interview and creates an English transcript for human review.
- The agent does not decide who is hired or rejected.
- The agent may answer only from this approved file and the approved FAQ source.
- If information is not available, Adeeb says that a recruiter can clarify it after the interview.

## Internship information
- Replace this section with verified details about the internship domains, learning objectives, remote or onsite option, expected work, duration, equipment, schedule, and application process.
- Do not add confidential client information, passwords, private phone numbers, payment accounts, or unapproved promises.

## Team and work process
- Add only approved information about mentoring, meeting cadence, expected deliverables, tools, and communication channels.

## Interview process
- The candidate identifies themselves after joining the private meeting link.
- The candidate can answer in English, Urdu, Hindi, or mixed speech.
- The candidate may say "repeat" or "next question".
- The agent can ask one short follow-up where the answer needs factual clarification.
- A human reviewer makes every hiring decision.
